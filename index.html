Ingredients

Essential

    • 2 eggs
    • 1 tbsp butter
    • 1 pinch salt
    • 1 pinch black pepper
    • 1 handful herbs

Optional

  • 1 tbsp milk
  • 30 g cheese
  • 30 g vegetables

Cooking

  1. Crack the eggs Tap each egg gently on a flat surface and open it into a bowl to avoid shell pieces.
  2. Whisk lightly Mix just until the yolks and whites come together. No need to overbeat.
  3. Season early Add salt and pepper now so the eggs are evenly seasoned from the start.
  4. Heat the pan Use low to medium-low heat. The pan should be warm, not hot.
  5. Add butter Let it melt on the warm pan. It should foam gently, not brown.
  6. Cook and stir Pour in the eggs and move them slowly with a spatula until soft curds form.
  7. Fold the omelette When mostly set but still soft, fold it gently in half.
  8. Serve and finish Slide onto a plate and sprinkle with fresh herbs of your choice.
